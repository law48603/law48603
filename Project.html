Family Background

I'm always so entertained when people try to guess my ethnicity because they can never figure it out. Hawaiian or Native American are among the most common of the backgrounds I get, but all (sadly) incorrect (...being those ethnicities would be a dream). I am... drumroll please... half-Korean half-White. My mom is full Korean, born there and and everything, while my dad is a white man from California. No, I do not know how to speak Korean, nor understand it. In fact, my own Korean mom cannot even speak the language, although she can understand it somewhat. Some say it's sad we don't take advantage of our Korean background, but I'll give it to her, she moved to America when she was 4 years old, so you could say we are a pretty Americanized family.

Having two sisters and a stepbrother, being with my family is the best. I have an older sister who is a senior at UGA, a younger brother who is a senior in high school, and a younger sister who is a junior in high school. We are all SO close -- they are basically my best friends.
